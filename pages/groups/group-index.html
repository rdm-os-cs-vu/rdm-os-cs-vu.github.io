Research Groups

There are X groups in the department with in total X researchers (including PhD students), summing up to Z FTEs. Their research data ranges from linked data about cultural heritage to collected brain signals using the EEG devices, from human-robot interaction to mathematical proofs. Providing a department-level guideline that fulfills the needs of all is impossible. We therefore encourage each group to come up with their own guidelines for specific kinds of data.
